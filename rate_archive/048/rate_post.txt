### Bebe Rexha - All Your Fault Pts. 1 & 2

All Your Fault: Pts. 1 & 2 are EPs by American singer Bebe Rexha. They were released on February 17, 2017 and August 11, 2017, respectively. The EPs are primarily pop influenced. The EPs includes singles "I Got You" and "Meant to Be", which peaked at #2 on the Billboard Hot 100 Chart. Featured artists include G-Eazy, Ty Dolla $ign, Gucci Mane, 2 Chainz, Lil Wayne, Kranium and Florida Georgia Line. Mike Wass of the music blog Idolator gave the first EP four-and-a-half stars out of five, calling it "a testament to her brilliant songwriting and supernatural ability to sniff out a hook." The EPs charted at #51 and #33, respectively, on the Billboard 200 chart.

### Camila Cabello - Camila

Camila is the eponymous debut studio album by Cuban-American singer Camila Cabello. It was released on January 12, 2018. Work on the album began in January 2017, following Cabello's departure from Fifth Harmony. The project was initially announced as The Hurting. The Healing. The Loving. but was later changed to simply Camila. Primarily a pop record, it incorporates elements of Latin music and R&B. The album was executive-produced by Frank Dukes, with additional work from Skrillex and The Futuristics, among others. Cabello also worked with artists such as Sia Furler, Charli XCX, and Ed Sheeran on tracks that were later scrapped. The debut LP also features Young Thug on the hit song and first single Havana. Camila has been met with positive reviews from contemporary music critics, most of whom commended the Latin influences and ballads featured. The album went #1 on the Billboard 200 Chart.

### Dua Lipa - Dua Lipa (an album by Dua Lipa in collaboration with Dua Lipa for Dua Lipa)

Dua Lipa is the debut studio album by English singer Dua Lipa. It was released on June 2, 2017 by Warner Bros. Records. The lyrical themes revolve around her personal views of love, rising above, sex, and self-empowerment.
The album has spawned eight singles, including the UK top 40 singles "Be the One", "Hotter than Hell", "Blow Your Mind (Mwah)", "IDGAF" and "New Rules", with the last becoming Lipa's first number-one single in the UK. "New Rules" also reached the top 10 in the United States, peaking at number six. The album features R&B singer Miguel on the track "Lost in Your Light". The album received positive reviews from music critics and hit #27 on the Billboard 200 chart.

### Zara Larsson - So Good

So Good is the second studio album by Swedish singer and songwriter Zara Larsson, and her first released internationally. It was released on March 17, 2017, through Epic Records and TEN Music Group. The album was originally scheduled to be released in January 2017; however, it was delayed so that changes could be made to a number of tracks. Several successful singles were released to promote the album, including "Lush Life", "Never Forget You", and "Ain't My Fault". The album features Ty Dolla $ign, MNEK, Wizkid, and Clean Bandit. Larsson also worked with Stargate and Charlie Puth. The album charted at #26 on the Billboard 200 Chart.

# Rules and shit

In what has become a great /r/popheads tradition, the majority of these rules have been copied and pasted from a previous rate.

* Listen to each song and **assign each a score between 1 and 10**. Decimals are fine, but **please refrain from giving decimal scores that have two decimal spots.** 

6.7: OK

6.75: Absolutely unacceptable
 
* **Yes, you have to listen to and rank every song**. The program will not work unless each song has a score!

* Your scores should not be considered confidential!
 
* You may give **ONE** song a **0** and **ONE** song an **11**. By one, we mean that this is **one song total**, not one song per album. Please reserve these for your least favorite and most favorite tracks; excessive sabotage ruins rate results and is very annoying!
 
* You can change your scores at any time! Feel free to PM either me at any time.
 
* The program makes things a lot more efficient and reduces errors on our part, this does mean that scores need to be sent in a very specific way. The easiest way to make sure your scores follow the necessary format is to **use the pre-prepared link, found [right here](https://tinyurl.com/yc2pulaf). PLEASE USE THAT**. You can copy and paste it to a notepad file or something and fill in your scores there, but PLEASE PLEASE PLEASE PLEASE use that format to send in you scores.

* If you want to attach a comment to a specific song (which is encouraged!) write it right after your score. Make sure nothing is separating your score and your comment except for a space!

Acceptable:

>New Rules: 0 What is this garbage!

Unacceptable:

>New Rules: What is this garbage! 0
>
>New Rules: 0 - What is this garbage!
> 
>New Rules: 0 (What is this garbage!)
>
>New Rules: This is garbage, so I will give it a 0!

* If you want to attach a comment to an album, add a colon after the ALBUM name and put your comment there.

Acceptable:

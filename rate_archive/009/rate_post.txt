#Fever vs. In the Zone vs. Music

###Rules and Info

* Listen to every song listed below. There are some bonus tracks I've included, so check the list!
* Assign a score of 1-10 for each song. Decimals are fine, but not between 0 and 1, and 10 and 11.
* However, you are allowed to award one 0 to a song, and one 11 to a song. Use them wisely!
* Include comments if you want! It's not necessary, but I will include some in the reveal write-up.
* PM me (/u/Kaphox) the scores by **using the link provided below** (not compulsory to use link, but preferred)
* Deadline is New Year's Day! That's January 1st.
* I will reveal the results on Friday 6th and Saturday 7th of January, 2017.

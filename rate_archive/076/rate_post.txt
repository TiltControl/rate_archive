Popheads Worldvision - Scores Now Open!
https://www.reddit.com/r/popheads/comments/cx956z/popheads_worldvision_scores_now_open/

At long last, it is time to open the score submissions for Popheads Worldvision! Fifty users have selected songs for their countries, and it's up to you to pick which country gave us the best discovery. 

Scoring is pretty simple: pick a country to get 12 points for their song, then another for 10 points, 8 points, 7 points, and descending from there. For full scoring details, you can view [THIS](https://redd.it/cjgmyj) thread from the signups. Also, in the interest of fairer weighting, I've decided to abolish the separate VIP category and merge it with the participants category. The new merged category will account for 2/3 of the final points, with spectators taking up the remaining 1/3. **Remember that you cannot give your own country points!** 

Below is a table listing all of the countries and their corresponding users, artists and songs. I recommend using the [Spotify playlist](https://open.spotify.com/playlist/2Z42dF2iBNRxzz1279FbvX?si=2j51dt7gRZOD0n6WRpUWYg) to listen to all of the songs. I have individually linked to songs that are not on Spotify in the table below. Submit your scores using [THIS](https://forms.gle/patMfvBBJmoxd6AJA) Google form!

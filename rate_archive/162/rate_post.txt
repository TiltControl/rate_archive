And here are the rules copy pasted from a dozen other rates, with a few rate-specific additions:

* **If you are brand new to rates, I highly recommend watching [this video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iQ1SDlLZcpM) to get acclimated.**
* You must listen to and submit a score for every song. If you send a ballot that's missing a score, I will message you as soon as I notice to finish your ballot.
* Each song must be scored between a 1 and 10, with the exception of one 0 and one 11, reserved for your least favorite and favorite songs in the rate respectively. One decimal place (e.g. 9.2) is acceptable, any more and I will have to round your scores. You are not required to use your 0 or 11 if you do not want to.
* Everyone's scores and comments for every song will be revealed at the end of the rate, so do not think of them as confidential!
* Scores may be changed and comments may be added up to the official due date.

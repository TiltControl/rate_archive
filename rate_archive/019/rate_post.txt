**[The Writing’s on the Wall – Destiny’s Child [1999]](https://images.genius.com/c6181e14f76d70f8ad1ae138c67f4150.1000x1000x1.png)**

This group consisted of [LaTavia Roberson]( https://bossip.files.wordpress.com/2016/12/screen-shot-2016-12-29-at-9-57-12-am.png?w=700) , [LeToya Luckett]( http://girlthatsmysong.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/05/letoya-luckett.jpg) , [Beyoncé]( http://www.dumpaday.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/02/beyonce-super-bowl-pictures-funny.jpg) and [Kelly Rowland]( https://s-media-cache-ak0.pinimg.com/originals/3c/cd/3d/3ccd3d43175f80579324b79eb328921d.jpg) at the time. This was their second album and the last album with the group’s original line up of members. However, this is the album that broke them into the mainstream where they achieved much success. The album was nominated for six Grammys and has since then went 8x Platinum in the United States, 3x Platinum in the UK and 5x Platinum in Canada. 

**Album: [Spotify]( https://play.spotify.com/album/78r5vrR1Wf60JPgFQ1drwr?play=true&utm_source=open.spotify.com&utm_medium=open), [Pandora]( https://www.pandora.com/artist/destinys-child/writings-on-wall), [Tidal]( https://listen.tidal.com/album/1321114), [Apple Music / iTunes]( https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/the-writings-on-the-wall/id266809606) and [Youtube]( https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL19A80C76FE183024)**

**[Funky Divas – En Vogue [1992]](http://cps-static.rovicorp.com/3/JPG_500/MI0003/562/MI0003562752.jpg?partner=allrovi.com)**

This group consisted of Dawn Robinson, Maxine Jones, Cindy Herron and Terry Ellis at the time. This album has been credited by many for creating the great demand for R&B Girl Groups and inspiring all other R&B Girl Groups that followed after. It was their second album, has sold over 5 million copies worldwide and is their highest selling album to date.

**Album: [Spotify]( https://play.spotify.com/album/7d2qNq4zap02SoWdvr0caA?play=true&utm_source=open.spotify.com&utm_medium=open), [Pandora]( https://www.pandora.com/artist/en-vogue/funky-divas), [Tidal]( https://listen.tidal.com/album/10565400), [Apple Music / iTunes]( https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/funky-divas/id414127599) and [Youtube]( https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLqxmLVac77V0OCgcLpLd4oq5HBG2RyLkM)**

**[CrazySexyCool – TLC [1994]]( http://louderthanwar.com/wp-content/uploads/crazysexycool.jpg)**

This group consisted of “Chilli”, “T-Boz” and “Left Eye” at the time. It was their second album and the most successful album commercially and critically of the three in this rate. The album has sold over 14 million copies worldwide becoming the best-selling album by an American girl group, the only album by a girl group to be awarded diamond status in the US, featured in Rolling Stone’s list of The 500 Greatest Albums of All Time and nominated for six Grammy Awards.

**Album: [Spotify]( https://play.spotify.com/album/5eg56dCpFn32neJak2vk0f?play=true&utm_source=open.spotify.com&utm_medium=open), [Pandora]( https://www.pandora.com/artist/tlc/crazy-sexy-cool), [Tidal]( https://listen.tidal.com/album/667700), [Apple Music / iTunes]( https://itunes.apple.com/us/album/crazysexycool/id270246704) and [Youtube]( https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLQrS_yooAdIE6JigKF421mxxlcBQ4mnf3)**

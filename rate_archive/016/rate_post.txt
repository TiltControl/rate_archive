##\/r/popheads Fantasy Eurovision

I'm back with /u/berober04, now here to detail the voting process of the \/r/popheads Fantasy Eurovision. Voting will last from today up until April 29. After that, there will be a little gap for us to prepare for results day. For the results, we will be hosting another two-day event on [plug.dj](https://plug.dj/popheads) on the 5th and 6th of May. Every participant is required to vote on the other songs, and they cannot vote on themselves. A spectator vote will also be held after normal voting has ended. More info on that when the time comes!

---

##Voting Process

For voting, we will be using a similar system to the real-life contest. Everyone who submitted a song will be able to vote for their 10 favourite songs. This is how Fantasy Eurovision differs from the other /r/popheads rates - you cannot rate every song. You will have to choose your 10 favourite, and then distribute your votes accordingly. The votes you get are 12, 10, 8, 7, 6, 5, 4, 3, 2, 1. So, if you submit a song to the contest, you will have to decide which songs to give your points to.

**[If you also submitted a song, use this link to submit your votes by April 29 ](https://www.reddit.com/message/compose?to=%2Fr%2Fpopheadseurovision&subject=Voting&message=My%20Country%3A%20%0A%0A*%2012%3A%0A*%2010%3A%0A*%20%20%208%3A%0A*%20%20%207%3A%0A*%20%20%206%3A%0A*%20%20%205%3A%0A*%20%20%204%3A%0A*%20%20%203%3A%0A*%20%20%202%3A%0A*%20%20%201%3A)**

Next to the voting numbers, please list the **country** you want to give your vote to. Not the artist or song.
